Christopher Palacios

Christopher Palacios is an artist, designer and educator who lives and works in portland, oregon.

For over 20 years Palacios lived in San Francisco, California until 2005 when he became Visiting Assistant Professor of Graphic Desgin at Ohio University School of Art + Design.
